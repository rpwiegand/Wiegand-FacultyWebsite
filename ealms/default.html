Eagle Air Local Music Spotlight

Starting in November of 2022, on Friday's from 4p – 6p, I will host a local music spotlight program on Winthrop University's radio station, Eagle Air! You can listen using the TuneIn app. Eventually, we'll have a proper website, but for now I'll host some content here on my faculty page. The goal is to maintain a show schedule a local music gig calendar.


Contact & Submissions

To contact Eagle Air's Local Music Spotlight show, please send email to: localmusicspotlight@winthrop.edu

If you are a musician with a gig in the near future, let me know about it with this form: Eagle Air Local Music Gig Submission Form

If you are a local band and would like us to play your music on the show, email us about this, then we'll explain how music submission works: localmusicspotlight@winthrop.edu
