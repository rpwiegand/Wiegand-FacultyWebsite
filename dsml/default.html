Data Science & Machine Learning Group

The Department of Mathematics and the Department of Computer Science \& Quantitative Methods have put together a student group for students and faculty to engage in and discuss topics relating to data science and machine learning. All students and faculty are welcome.

This isn't a class! It is a student-led dialog about these topics, and our main focus is just increased engagement and fun! This semester, we're meeting every other Thursday in Thurmond 208 at 11a. Come on by!
