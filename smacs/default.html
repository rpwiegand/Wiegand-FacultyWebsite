Seminar on Mathematics & Computer Science

The Department of Mathematics and the Department of Computing & Information Sciences have put together a student group for students and faculty to engage in and discuss topics relating to the intersection of math and computer science. All students and faculty are welcome.

This isn't a class! It is a student-led dialog about these topics, and our main focus is increased engagement and fun! This semester, we're meeting every other Thursday in Owens 108 at 11a. Come on by!
